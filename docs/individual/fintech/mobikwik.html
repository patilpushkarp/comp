Mobikwik#

Mobikwik is a fintech focusing on mobile wallets and Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) services. Main services include -

  1. Phone and DTH recharge

  2. Bill Payments

  3. Shopping in local stores

  4. Transfer money to bank
